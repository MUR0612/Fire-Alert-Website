Bushfire Survival Plan
Have a written and practiced bushfire survival plan for your family, including evacuation routes and meeting points.
Home Preparation
Clear gutters, remove flammable materials from around your home, and seal vents to prevent ember entry.
Evacuation Route Confirmed
Know the safest route out and prepare your vehicle for a quick departure.
